Crude Fish Oil

Origin :Indonesia
Type :Conventional
Application :For Industrial

Crude Fish Oil PT Adi Alam Segar (Alam Group Indonesia)

Harvested from the Depths, Nourishing Excellence: Crude Fish Oil - Nature's Elixir for Livestock Wellness, Crafted with Precision by Alam Group Indonesia.

Delve into the ocean of superior livestock nutrition with Crude Fish Oil by Alam Group Indonesia, where every drop encapsulates the essence of marine richness and purity. Sourced from premium fish species, our crude fish oil is a testament to our commitment to elevating animal health and well-being.

Crude Fish Oil, revered for its omega-3 fatty acids and nutrient-rich composition, stands as a beacon in our dedication to providing high-quality animal nutrition. Extracted from a variety of meticulously selected fish, each drop symbolizes the precision and authenticity that define Alam Group Indonesia.

Derived from our responsibly sourced fish stocks, Crude Fish Oil undergoes a cutting-edge extraction process, ensuring the retention of essential nutrients. Packed with omega-3 fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals, our crude fish oil serves as a potent dietary supplement, promoting the overall health and productivity of livestock.

Embrace the Benefits

Enhance your well-being with Crude Fish Oil, a natural source of essential omega-3 fatty acids that supports heart health, cognitive function, and overall vitality, making it an ideal addition to your daily nutritional routine.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids for Vitality: Crude Fish Oil is a natural source of omega-3 fatty acids, promoting heart health, immune system function, and overall vitality in livestock. Enhanced Growth and Reproduction: The nutrient-rich composition of crude fish oil contributes to improved growth rates and reproductive performance in livestock, supporting herd or flock development. Coat and Skin Health: Omega-3 fatty acids in fish oil nourish the coat and skin, resulting in a glossy and healthy appearance in animals. Improved Feed Palatability: The natural flavors of crude fish oil enhance feed palatability, encouraging consistent consumption and ensuring animals receive the full spectrum of nutritional benefits.
